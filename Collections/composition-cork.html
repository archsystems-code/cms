Captivating proportions and colorations are created by state of the art technology in the newest collection -  Composition Cork, which includes the sound-absorbing Shapes,  Rounds,  Bricks and Sheets. The Shapes grouping has three-dimensional patterns and a restyled palette which can be mixed and matched to achieve warmth and texture while providing a sustainable and distinctive design statement.  Bricks are precision cut in preselected sizes and thicknesses for a fresh artisanal appeal. Rounds, made from recycled wine corks, have the added benefit of slip resistance and can be used for interior and exterior applications including flooring, walls, and backsplashes. The material can also be stained or grouted in any color for a luxurious retro choice.
